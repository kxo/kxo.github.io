Arizona's Finest Italian Restaurant & Pizzeria!

Strombolli's Restaurant is Arizona's finest family-friendly Italian Restaurant & Pizzeria. Based in the pine forest town of Flagstaff beaneath the San Francisco peaks, we've been serving up the finest range of Italian food including our famous stone-baked pizzas and packed calzones since 1994.
